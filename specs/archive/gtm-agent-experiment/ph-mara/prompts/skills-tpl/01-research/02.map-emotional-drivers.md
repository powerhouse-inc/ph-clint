# RS.02 Map Emotional Drivers

Prerequisite: Audience (RS.00) and product analysis (RS.01) complete.

## RS.02.1 List audience concerns by emotional intensity

- Identify 5-7 concerns the audience has, ordered by what they FEEL first (not what's logically most important)
- For each concern: state it in the audience's own words (not marketing language)
- For each concern: why is it urgent RIGHT NOW?
- For each concern: what are they afraid of getting wrong?

## RS.02.2 Map product responses to each driver

- For each emotional driver, how does the product address it?
- Be honest when the response is partial ("we address this, but not completely")
- Note where the product's response is genuinely strong vs. adequate vs. aspirational

## RS.02.3 Define the narrative arc

- Which concern do you lead with? (Usually the most urgent, not the most differentiated)
- How do you build trust through the middle drivers?
- Where do you ask for action? (After enough trust is established, not before)
- What's the emotional journey: urgency → caution → recognition → trust → action?

### Expected Scenario Outcome

`research/emotional-drivers.md` exists with 5-7 drivers ordered by emotional intensity, product responses mapped, and narrative arc defined.
