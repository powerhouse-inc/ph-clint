# Expected Skill Outcome

The `research/` directory contains a complete positioning brief:

- `product-analysis.md` — What we have and what we're missing
- `emotional-drivers.md` — The narrative arc from concern to trust
- `swot.md` — Honest assessment of position
- `vendor-checklist.md` — How we'd score on a standard vendor evaluation

This brief is the foundation. No messaging, design, or production work begins until it is reviewed and approved.
