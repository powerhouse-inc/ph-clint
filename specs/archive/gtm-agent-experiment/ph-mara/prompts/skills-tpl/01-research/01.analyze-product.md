# RS.01 Analyze the Product

Prerequisite: Audience confirmed in RS.00.

## RS.01.1 Map capabilities to audience concerns

- For each audience concern identified in RS.00, determine which product capabilities address it
- Distinguish between "genuinely differentiated" (competitors can't easily replicate) and "table stakes" (expected by the market)
- Note capabilities that are impressive but don't map to any audience concern — these should not lead the messaging

## RS.01.2 Identify gaps honestly

- What audience concerns does the product NOT address yet?
- What's immature or incomplete? Be specific about the gap and its impact.
- What would a skeptical evaluator point to as a weakness?

## RS.01.3 Survey existing positioning

- Review any existing marketing sites, partner sites, documentation
- What angle are they taking? What are they emphasizing?
- What's the gap in existing positioning that this new package should fill?
- Note any content that should NOT be duplicated

### Expected Scenario Outcome

`research/product-analysis.md` exists with capabilities mapped to audience concerns, honest gaps identified, and existing positioning surveyed.
